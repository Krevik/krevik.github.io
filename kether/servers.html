What's different about this server?

  • Commands
    • /mix command when you want to randomly shuffle teams
    • /r /nr command when you want to announce that you're ready or not ready
    • /cur /current /tank /witch /boss commands to check current map progress and bosses spawn points
    • /setscores (arg1) (arg2) - you can call a vote to change actual teams scoring
    • /afk /spec /spectate /join - team management commands
  • Bugfixes
    • Double getup animation after SI punch you or incapitate you should not occur
    • When survivor is in getting up after punch state, he will not be pushed by other SI attack
    • You won't be able to get up somebody and heal at the same time
    • There is "God Frames Control" installed on the server - the plugin that controls situation when survivor is temporary immortal after SI attack (for. eg. after hunter jump while survivor is in spit)
    • There should not occur bug when survivor is hit by charger and jockey at the same time
    • Ladders should work better and should not stop you when you jump near them
    • Lags are compensated in better way
    • After map change spectators should keep their team
    • Jockey unsilencer - no more silent jockeys
    • Improved special items passing - adrenaline, pills
    • Tank should not throw rocks that trajectory is calculated in a wrong way - it should improve tank rock accuracy
    • Bugfix about badly set player's health after being punch by prop (like car)
    • Witch can chase player inside the safezone now
    • Witch should not be triggered twice now
    • Witch should not attack SI now, and should not loose her target so easily
  • Other changes/improvements
    • As special infected (SI) when you despawn (respawn) you gain half of the missing HP back
    • Tank rock lag is compensated in better way so it's easier to hit
    • On boss kill (or at the round end) chat will show damage done to boss by certain players
    • On survivor death his secondary weapon will be dropped too (like melee weapon)
    • During tank, no spitter will be spawned
    • Special infected (SI) do not have shadow anymore that makes them visible through some walls
    • Skill Detect Plugin - announcements in chat about skeets, high pounces, etc
    • Spectators rate is set to 30 to avoid redundant server resources usage
    • At the end of the round survivor damage statistics will be print to the chat (MVP,LVP)
